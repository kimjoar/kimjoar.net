Davidsson & Klofsten, 
	Business Platform Model
	Hjørnesteiner
		Forretningsidé - can be communicated internally and externally
		Produkt - capable of satisfying the markets’ needs and wants
		Marked - large enough and profitable enough to ensure survival
		Organisasjon - existence of an organizational structure that facilitates functional co-ordination
		Kjernegruppe-ekspertise - crucial to have access to expertise for solving the firm's real problems
		Kjernegruppe-motivasjon - at least one person is highly motivated and that the other key actors are committed to the business idea
		Kunderelasjon - A customer base must be qualitatively and quantitatively strong enough to generate operating revenue.
		Andre relasjoner - these relations complement the customer relationships
